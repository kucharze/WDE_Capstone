How to play Magic the Gathering

Magic the Gathering is a table top card game where players act as powerful sourcers called Planeswalkers. Each Player starts with 20 life. Once a player reduces their opponents life total to 0, they are the winner. A player can also win when their opponent runs out of cards in their deck.

Players take turns drawing cards from their deck. The cards in the deck. Each turn, players can play one land card from there hand. Lands are used as fuel to cast spells. There are differnt types of spells. (See Learn about cards page for more info.)

There are several different stages to each turn

  1. Upkeep
  2. Main phase
  3. Combat
  4. Main phase 2
  5. End of Turn
During the upkeep phase, players untap all their spells on the field and draw a card. During the Main phase or Main phase 2, players can play any number of spells from their hand. During Combat, players can attack with their creatures. The defending player will have the opprotunity to block with their creatures.
