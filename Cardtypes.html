Learn about different types of cards

Creature

Creatures are the most basic type of card. They posses a cost, power, and toughness.

Some creatures will even have effects. Some can trigger on their own at specific times, while others you can activate on your own.

Sourcery

Sourceries are a form of spells. You can cast sourceries at any time on your main phase

Instant

Instants are like sourcery spells, however, you are capable of playing them on your opponent's turn.

Artifact

This is the fourth item's accordion body.

Enchantment

This is the Fifth item's accordion body.

Planeswalkers

Planeswalkers are the most advanced type of card.
