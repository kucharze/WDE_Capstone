Learn about different types of cards

Creature

Creatures are the most basic type of card. They posses a cost, power, and toughness.

Some creatures will even have effects. Some can trigger on their own at specific times, while others you can activate on your own.

Sourcery

Sourceries are a form of spells. You can cast sourceries at any time on your main phase or main phase 2.

Some sourceries will need additional costs that are described in addition to their normal cost. You can see an example on the picture shown above.

Sourceries can provide a wide range of effects. These can range from killing an opponents creature, to helping you gain life.

Instant

Instants are like sourcery spells, however, you are capable of playing them on your opponent's turn. This helps you to react to what your opponent is doing.

Other types of spells can have an ability called flash. This lets you cast them at any time you could cast an instant.

Artifact

Artifacts are a type of permanent that sit's on the battlefielf and applies an effect. It is also possible for a creature to also be an artifact.

Enchantment

Enchantments are similiar to Artifacts, in which they sit on the battlefield and apply an effect. It is also possible for a creature to also be an enchantment.

Planeswalkers

Planeswalkers are the most advanced type of card. Planeswalkers starts with so many loyalty counters. You can then add or remove loyalty from them in order to activate their abilities.

You must be careful though. It is possible for your opponent to attack you Planeswalkers and deal them damage, removing their loyalty in the process.
